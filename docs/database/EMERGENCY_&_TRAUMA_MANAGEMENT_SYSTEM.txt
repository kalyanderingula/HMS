# Enterprise Hospital Management System (HMS)

# Emergency & Trauma Management System Documentation

---

# 1. Module Overview

The Emergency & Trauma Management System is the real-time emergency response, trauma stabilization, and acute patient management backbone of the hospital management system.

This module manages:

* Emergency triage workflows
* Ambulance arrivals
* Walk-in emergency registration
* Trauma case management
* Emergency bed assignments
* Resuscitation workflows
* Emergency procedures
* Critical care stabilization
* Emergency laboratory and radiology workflows
* Disaster management
* Emergency billing and insurance
* Sentinel event tracking
* Emergency audit and compliance

This module integrates with:

* EMR/EHR
* ICU & Critical Care
* Surgery & OT
* Laboratory Systems
* Radiology/PACS
* Pharmacy Systems
* Ambulance Systems
* Smart Emergency Devices
* Billing & Insurance

---

# 2. Enterprise Architecture Principles

This schema follows enterprise emergency care standards:

* UUID-based primary keys
* Real-time emergency workflows
* High-availability architecture
* Mass casualty support
* Trauma registry support
* Code blue workflows
* Disaster recovery readiness
* Audit-ready design
* Multi-hospital emergency support
* Enterprise scalability
