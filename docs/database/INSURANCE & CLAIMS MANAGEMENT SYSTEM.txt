# Enterprise Hospital Management System (HMS)

# Insurance & Claims Management System Documentation

---

# 1. Module Overview

The Insurance & Claims Management System is the financial authorization, payer integration, reimbursement, and revenue cycle management backbone of the hospital management system.

This module manages:

* Insurance provider management
* Insurance plan configuration
* Patient insurance policies
* Eligibility verification
* Prior authorization workflows
* Insurance claims management
* Electronic claims submission
* EDI transactions
* Claim denial management
* Appeals and resubmissions
* Reimbursement tracking
* Revenue integrity validation
* Fraud detection
* Insurance analytics and reporting
* Audit and compliance tracking

This module integrates with:

* Billing & Financial Management
* EMR/EHR
* Patient Management
* Laboratory Systems
* Pharmacy Systems
* Surgery & OT
* Emergency Management
* ICD/CPT coding systems
* Clearinghouses
* Accounting systems

---

# 2. Enterprise Architecture Principles

This schema follows enterprise insurance and revenue cycle standards:

* UUID-based primary keys
* Multi-payer support
* EDI transaction readiness
* HIPAA-compliant workflows
* Denial management automation
* Revenue integrity architecture
* Audit-ready design
* High-availability claims workflows
* Multi-hospital insurance support
* Enterprise scalability
