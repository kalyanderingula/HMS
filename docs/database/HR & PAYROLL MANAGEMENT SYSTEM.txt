# Enterprise Hospital Management System (HMS)

# HR & Payroll Management System Documentation

---

# 1. Module Overview

The HR & Payroll Management System is the workforce management, employee lifecycle, payroll processing, credentialing, compliance, and organizational management backbone of the hospital management system.

This module manages:

* Employee master records
* Organizational structure
* Recruitment and onboarding
* Attendance and rostering
* Leave management
* Payroll processing
* Benefits and reimbursements
* Performance reviews
* Training and competency tracking
* Employee compliance tracking
* Vaccination tracking
* Workforce analytics
* HR audit and compliance

This module integrates with:

* Doctor Management
* Nursing Management
* ICU & Critical Care
* Payroll & Finance Systems
* Biometric Devices
* Compliance Systems
* E-learning Platforms
* Security & Access Systems
* ERP Platforms

---

# 2. Enterprise Architecture Principles

This schema follows enterprise HR and workforce standards:

* UUID-based primary keys
* Enterprise organizational hierarchy
* Workforce scalability
* Payroll automation
* Compliance-ready architecture
* Biometric attendance readiness
* Audit-ready HR tracking
* Multi-hospital workforce support
* Credential lifecycle management
* Enterprise-grade analytics

# TABLE: employees

## Purpose

Stores core employee master records.

Acts as the central workforce identity table.

* One employee can have attendance records.
* One employee can have qualifications.
* One employee can belong to departments.

| employee_id          | UUID         | Primary key               |
