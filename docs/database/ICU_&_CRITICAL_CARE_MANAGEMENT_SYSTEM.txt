# Enterprise Hospital Management System (HMS)

# ICU & Critical Care Management System Documentation

---

# 1. Module Overview

The ICU & Critical Care Management System is the real-time life-support, monitoring, and critical patient management backbone of the hospital management system.

This module manages:

* ICU admissions and transfers
* ICU bed management
* Ventilator management
* Hemodynamic monitoring
* Cardiac monitoring
* Medication titration
* Fluid balance management
* Critical alerts
* Code blue events
* Resuscitation tracking
* Device integrations
* Telemetry streams
* Family counseling
* Mortality reviews
* Audit and compliance

This module integrates with:

* EMR/EHR
* Nursing Management
* Laboratory Systems
* Pharmacy Systems
* Bedside Monitors
* Ventilators
* Infusion Pumps
* Telemetry Systems
* HL7/FHIR Systems

---

# 2. Enterprise Architecture Principles

This schema follows enterprise ICU standards:

* UUID-based primary keys
* Real-time critical care monitoring
* High-frequency telemetry support
* Multi-device integrations
* High-availability architecture
* ICU workflow optimization
* Patient safety compliance
* Audit-ready design
* Enterprise scalability
* Multi-hospital ICU support
