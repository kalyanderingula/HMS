# Enterprise Hospital Management System (HMS)

# Rehabilitation & Physiotherapy Management Module Documentation

---

# 1. Module Overview

The Rehabilitation & Physiotherapy module manages patient therapy referrals, assessments, treatment plans, therapy sessions, and progress tracking.

This module manages:

* Rehabilitation specialties
* Therapist registry
* Doctor referrals for therapy
* Functional assessments
* Treatment plan creation
* Session scheduling and documentation
* Progress tracking
* Billing
* Equipment tracking

This module integrates with:

* Patient Management
* EMR/EHR
* Doctor Management
* Admission Management
* Billing
* Surgery (post-op rehab)

# 2. Tables

---

# TABLE: rehab_specialties

## Example Values

* Physiotherapy, Occupational Therapy, Speech Therapy, Cardiac Rehab

---

# TABLE: rehab_therapists

## Purpose

Stores therapist registry linked to employees.

---

# TABLE: rehab_referrals

## Purpose

Stores doctor referrals for rehabilitation services.

---

# TABLE: rehab_assessments

## Purpose

Stores initial and periodic functional assessments.

---

# TABLE: rehab_treatment_plans

## Purpose

Stores structured therapy plans with frequency and goals.

---

# TABLE: rehab_sessions

## Purpose

Stores individual therapy sessions with exercises performed and progress notes.

---

# TABLE: rehab_progress_records

## Purpose

Tracks improvement metrics over time.

---

# TABLE: rehab_equipment

## Purpose

Tracks rehab department equipment inventory.

---

# TABLE: rehab_billing

## Purpose

Stores per-session billing records.
