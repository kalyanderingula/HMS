# Enterprise Hospital Management System (HMS)

# Surgery & Operation Theatre (OT) Management System Documentation

---

# 1. Module Overview

The Surgery & Operation Theatre (OT) Management System is the surgical workflow, perioperative coordination, and operation theatre management backbone of the hospital management system.

This module manages:

* OT room management
* Surgery scheduling
* Surgical team coordination
* Preoperative assessments
* Anesthesia workflows
* Intraoperative documentation
* Implant tracking
* Surgical instrument tracking
* Recovery room workflows
* Sterilization management
* Surgical billing
* Robotic surgery integration
* Surgical quality metrics
* Audit and compliance

This module integrates with:

* Patient Management
* EMR/EHR
* ICU & Critical Care
* Nursing Management
* Pharmacy
* CSSD
* Laboratory Systems
* PACS/Radiology
* Billing & Insurance
* Smart OT devices

---

# 2. Enterprise Architecture Principles

This schema follows enterprise surgical standards:

* UUID-based primary keys
* Multi-OT support
* Real-time surgery scheduling
* Sterility and instrument traceability
* Implant tracking
* WHO surgical safety workflows
* Audit-ready architecture
* High-availability OT coordination
* Enterprise scalability
* Multi-hospital OT support
