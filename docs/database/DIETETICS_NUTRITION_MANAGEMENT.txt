# Enterprise Hospital Management System (HMS)

# Dietetics & Nutrition Management Module Documentation

---

# 1. Module Overview

The Dietetics & Nutrition Management module handles patient dietary planning, nutritional assessments, meal ordering, kitchen management, and food safety compliance.

This module manages:

* Diet type classification
* Patient diet orders
* Nutritional assessments and screening
* Meal planning and delivery
* Intake monitoring
* Kitchen menu management
* Food safety compliance
* Audit tracking

This module integrates with:

* Patient Management
* Admission & Bed Management
* EMR/EHR
* Nursing Management
* Billing

---

# 2. Tables

---

# TABLE: diet_types

## Purpose

Stores diet classifications.

## Example Values

* Regular, Diabetic, Renal, Cardiac, Liquid, Soft, NPO

---

# TABLE: meal_types

## Purpose

Stores meal time categories.

## Example Values

* Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack

---

# TABLE: allergen_types

## Purpose

Stores food allergens.

## Example Values

* Gluten, Dairy, Nuts, Shellfish

---

# TABLE: patient_diet_orders

## Purpose

Stores doctor-prescribed diet orders for patients.

## Relationships

* One patient can have multiple diet orders across admissions.

---

# TABLE: diet_plan_items

## Purpose

Stores individual food items in a diet plan.

---

# TABLE: nutritional_assessments

## Purpose

Stores detailed nutritional evaluations.

---

# TABLE: nutritional_screening

## Purpose

Stores initial malnutrition risk screening.

## Example Tools

* MUST, NRS-2002, SGA

---

# TABLE: meal_orders

## Purpose

Tracks daily meal orders per patient.

---

# TABLE: meal_intake_records

## Purpose

Records how much patient consumed.

---

# TABLE: kitchen_menu

## Purpose

Stores daily kitchen menu by diet type and meal.

---

# TABLE: food_safety_logs

## Purpose

Tracks food safety inspections.

---

# High-Level Relationships

```text
patient_diet_orders
│
├── diet_plan_items
└── meal_orders
      └── meal_intake_records

nutritional_assessments → patients
nutritional_screening → patients
```
