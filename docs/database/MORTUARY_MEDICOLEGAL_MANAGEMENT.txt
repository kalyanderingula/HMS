# Enterprise Hospital Management System (HMS)

# Mortuary & Medico-Legal Management Module Documentation

---

# 1. Module Overview

The Mortuary & Medico-Legal module handles death registration, body storage, release, autopsy, and medico-legal case management.

This module manages:

* Death record registration
* Death certificate generation
* Mortuary unit and compartment tracking
* Body storage and release
* Autopsy requests and findings
* Medico-legal case (MLC) registration
* MLC examination documentation
* Police/legal coordination documents
* Audit compliance

This module integrates with:

* Patient Management
* Admission Management
* EMR/EHR
* Emergency & Trauma
* Security & Compliance

---

# 2. Tables

---

# TABLE: mortuary_units

## Purpose

Stores mortuary infrastructure (capacity, occupancy).

---

# TABLE: death_records

## Purpose

Stores patient death registration.

## Key Fields

* cause_of_death, manner_of_death, certifying_doctor, certificate_number

---

# TABLE: body_storage_records

## Purpose

Tracks body placement in mortuary compartments.

---

# TABLE: body_release_records

## Purpose

Tracks body handover to family/authority.

---

# TABLE: autopsy_records

## Purpose

Stores autopsy findings and reports.

---

# TABLE: medico_legal_cases

## Purpose

Stores MLC registration (assault, accident, poisoning, etc.).

---

# TABLE: mlc_examination_records

## Purpose

Stores doctor's medico-legal examination findings.

---

# TABLE: mlc_documents

## Purpose

Stores legal documents (FIR copy, wound certificate, etc.).

---

# High-Level Relationships
